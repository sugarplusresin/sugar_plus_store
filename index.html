FAQ & Resin Care

  • Only domestic delivery in Singapore available.
  • Waiting time from order being confirmed to delivery is typically 1-2 weeks.
  • Keychains are handmade hence please understand that there would be small imperfections.
How to care for your resin keychain:
  • Although resin is resillient, scratches may build up over time and cause the piece to look a bit scuffed. Hence, try to avoid bumping or scratching your keychain.
  • Avoid storing your keychain in direct sunlight for long periods of time as this may lead to the resin softening or yellowing.
  • If necessary, clean your resin keychain using mild soap such as dish soap and water to remove any oil and dirt built up on its surface.
